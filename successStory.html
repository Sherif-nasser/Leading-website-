RME Success Story

Rowad Modern Engineering is one of the top construction companies in Egypt. With over two thousand employees, they are rapidly growing in Egypt and Africa region as well. Communication is a key competency for all their projects and hence they wanted to focus on written communication.

Leading 365 has successfully designed a course on Business and Report Writing for RME staff and team leaders to meet their needs.

Successfully delivered 20 rounds of Business and Report Writing to junior, senior and team leaders.

Workshop offered virtually due to pandemic restrictions.
